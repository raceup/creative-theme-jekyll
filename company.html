Vision

Make the purest, most exhilarating driving experience available to everybody.

Mission

We build electric hill climb racers for people who seek a fun, uncompromised and accessible experience and we do this with a pioneering spirit, keeping fate in our values of innovation and sustainability.

Business presentation

Race UP is an Italian startup based in Imola that designs, manufactures and sells an electric single seater especially designed for hill climb races and competitions. Such races are traditionally short, take place in high altitude locations and require superb handling and acceleration. These characteristics led the design of our car and made us adopt an electric power-train.
We want to deliver a fulfilling and uncompromised experience and our commitment toward this objective goes above and beyond the product itself, we want to make our customers’ life easier during the entire utilization period, reducing as much as possible the burdens and shortcomings traditionally faced by motor heads trying to approach the world of hill climb races. This, of course, must start with the price of the car: we plan on selling our single seater at an extremely competitive price for an electric vehicle, making the adoption of this technology as accessible as it’s ever been. This approach continues after the selling of the product: on top of ensuring a 360 degrees post sales service and a comprehensive assistance, we will organize various events and competitions specifically thought for our car, during which we’ll be taking care of the transportation of the vehicles and overall logistics, thus making the usage experience a truly effortless and enjoyable one. Our value proposition is clear: we are stripping the burdensome, weighty, unnecessary part from the motorsport experience to deliver the purer, more rewarding more fulfilling part of it.
